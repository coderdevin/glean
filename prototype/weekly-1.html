Agent、Edge、和正在重写的浏览器 Agents, the edge, and a browser being rewritten

本周双线：模型厂商把工作流打包成技能；基础设施把调函数做成默认。还有第三条暗线 — 浏览器层在被悄悄重写。 Two threads: vendors packaging workflows as skills; infra making function-calls the default. A quieter third — the browser layer is being rewritten.

2026-05-18 → 2026-05-24May 18 – May 24, 2026 8 篇8 picks 约 24 分钟~24 min
编辑首语 / linyi & mei

这一周最大的反差是节奏。Anthropic 发了 Skills，几乎不带营销噪音；Cloudflare 把 RPC 加进 Workers 时也只是发了一篇博客和一个 changelog——但圈内的工程师群消息明显多起来。两件事的共通之处是，它们都在做"减法"：减少胶水代码，让你更直接地表达"我要做什么"。

另一边，Dan Abramov 写了一篇 RSC 一年回顾，给了过去一年困扰大家的一个边界问题更准的描述：边界其实是数据时效。读完会想去重审一遍自己手上的项目。

第三条暗线是基础设施层在被悄悄重写——CSS @scope 终于全可用，Bun 把 monorepo 装包压到秒级，SQLite 的 query planner 改了。我们用 5 个章节把这些线索捋顺。

Editor's note / linyi & mei

The most striking contrast this week was tempo. Anthropic shipped Skills with almost no marketing noise; Cloudflare added RPC to Workers in a single blog post — but engineering chats lit up. Both moves are subtractive: less glue, more direct expression of intent.

On a separate beat, Dan Abramov's RSC retrospective reframed the boundary that's been confusing people for a year: it isn't client vs server — it's data freshness. Worth re-auditing whatever you're building.

A third, quieter thread: the infra layer is being rewritten — CSS @scope ships everywhere, Bun pushes monorepo installs into the low seconds, SQLite quietly rewrites its query planner. We've grouped this week into five sections.
