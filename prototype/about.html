一份双语技术刊 A bilingual tech zine

想让自己的工作小组每周一开会前，读到一份比 timeline 更靠谱的精选。做着做着可以公开了，于是有了这里。 I wanted my work team to read something better than the timeline before Monday standup. Then I realized it could be public.

每天扫 100+ 源，挑 1–3 条；周一汇总成一期。AI 起草，我把关。选稿和点评始终由人。 Scan 100+ sources daily, pick 1–3. Numbered weekly on Mondays. AI drafts, I approve. Selection and notes stay human.
