2 · AI 在我们流程里能做什么2 · What AI is allowed to do

DeepSeek 在我们流程里只做三件事：抓正文、双语翻译、起草要点。它不决定选稿，不写编辑点评，不排序周刊。它的草稿在编辑面板里全部是可编辑的，发布前会被人改一遍——平均 60% 的卡片会被改至少一处。DeepSeek does exactly three things: extracts the body, translates both ways, drafts bullet points. It does not select picks, write editor's notes, or order the weekly. Its drafts are all editable in our admin panel — on average 60% of cards get at least one human edit before publish.

AI · YES

起翻译草稿Translation draft

英中互译的第一稿。编辑会校对术语和语气。First-pass EN↔ZH. A human checks term & tone.

AI · NO

编辑点评Editor's note

"为什么读这一条"始终由人写。AI 永远不替编辑表态。"Why read this" is always human-written. AI never takes a stance for us.

AI · NO

选稿决策Selection

AI 可以给候选打 importance/novelty 分供编辑参考，但最终选 / 拒由编辑按下按钮。AI may score candidates as a reference. Pick / reject is always a human click.

AI · YES

标签建议Tag suggest

从现有 tag 表里选 1-3 个。编辑可全部替换。Picks 1–3 from existing taxonomy. Editor may replace all.

3 · 利益冲突3 · Conflicts of interest

如果编辑或贡献者跟某条新闻的作者 / 公司有雇佣 / 顾问 / 投资关系，会在那条卡片底部加一行 编辑披露。我们不收任何形式的"推广费"——如果有，会在 /about 公告。If an editor or contributor has an employment / advisory / investment relationship with the author or company of a pick, the card carries an editor's disclosure line at the bottom. We take zero paid promotion — and we'd announce it on /about if we ever did.

4 · 纠错与下架4 · Corrections & takedowns

发现错误？发邮件到 corrections@glean.team。
技术细节有误 24 小时内修订并在文末标注；事实性错误重发勘误。 Spot something wrong? Email corrections@glean.team.
Technical mis-statements fixed within 24h with a footnote; factual errors trigger a corrigendum.

5 · 做不到的事5 · What I won't pretend to do

这不是"全栈技术媒体"。覆盖系统 · 工具 · 设计三个领域；硬件 / 学术论文 / 创业新闻 不在范围内。This isn't full-stack tech media. Coverage: systems · tooling · design. Hardware, academic papers, startup news — out of scope.
