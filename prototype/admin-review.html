Durable Objects now ship cross-region replicas

blog.cloudflare.com · by Greg McKeon · Mar 22, 2026

Today we are announcing the general availability of cross-region replicas for Durable Objects. Strong consistency is preserved while reads automatically route to the nearest replica…

For the past 18 months we've been testing a new replication topology that allows DOs to operate across regions while maintaining the strong consistency guarantees customers rely on. Today, those replicas leave beta and are available to all customers on Workers paid plans.

Behind the scenes we use a modified Raft consensus where the leader's region is configurable per-object. Writes route to the leader; reads happen at the nearest replica that has caught up within a freshness window you specify (default: 50ms).

Enabling replication requires a single addition to your wrangler.toml:

[[durable_objects.bindings]]
name = "ROOMS"
class_name = "Room"
script_name = "rooms"
replicas = "global"

Writes still incur the round-trip to your leader region, but reads now hit the nearest colo with cached state…
